# Research Brief: Garden Landscape — Nordic Cluster Expansion

## The Garden — Context

The Garden is a project to prototype planetary governance through play, simulation, and embodied experience. We are mapping actors across four orientations:

- **Garden of Eden**: Living systems, ecology, land stewardship, regenerative economics, indigenous knowledge
- **Spaceship Earth**: Technology, infrastructure, governance tools, alternative ownership, digital twins
- **Eleusinian Mysteries**: Transformative games, LARP, ritual, experiential futures, festival culture
- **General Assembly of Earth**: Global governance innovation, participatory democracy, rights of nature, climate justice

## Objective

Conduct a **Nordic-focused landscape scan** for actors in Sweden, Denmark, Norway, Finland, Iceland, and the Baltics that we may be missing. The Nordic region is where The Garden's first prototype will be built, so deep coverage here is critical.

## What We Need

### Priority 1: Nordic ASSEMBLY actors (governance, democracy, rights)
Find Swedish/Nordic organisations working on:
- Citizens' assemblies, deliberative democracy, sortition
- Rights of nature legislation in Nordic countries
- Climate governance, planetary boundaries policy
- Long-term governance, future generations institutions
- Nordic governance innovation labs, policy institutes
- Sami/indigenous governance and rights movements

### Priority 2: Nordic GARDEN actors (ecology, land, regeneration)
Find Swedish/Nordic organisations working on:
- Regenerative agriculture, agroforestry, permaculture
- Rewilding, ecosystem restoration, biodiversity
- Community land trusts, commons-based land management
- Water stewardship, marine conservation
- Traditional ecological knowledge, Sami land practices
- Food systems transformation, urban farming

### Priority 3: Nordic SPACESHIP actors (tech, governance tools)
Find Swedish/Nordic organisations working on:
- GovTech, civic tech, digital democracy platforms
- Open data, environmental monitoring, digital twins
- Cooperative platforms, alternative ownership models
- Blockchain/web3 for sustainability
- AI for governance, participatory budgeting tools

### Priority 4: Cross-cutting Nordic actors
- Nordic foundations funding governance/ecology/simulation work
- Universities with relevant research groups
- Festival/event organisers bridging art and governance
- Networks connecting Nordic civil society with global movements
