# Domain Name Suggestions

The project maps organizations across four orientations — ecology, technology, transformative experience, and governance — to prototype planetary governance through play, simulation, and embodied experience. Names like "Garden Crawler" and "Garden Landscape" skew too heavily toward the ecology orientation.

## Top Picks

1. **protogov.org** — Short, memorable, captures "prototyping governance." The "proto" prefix covers the experimental/play dimension while "gov" anchors it. Doesn't exclude ecology, tech, or ritual.
2. **planetarylens.org** — The "lens" metaphor directly maps to how the four orientations work as complementary ways of seeing.
3. **planetassembly.org** — Mirrors "General Assembly of Earth" while being broad enough to house all four lenses.
4. **govatlas.org** — Emphasizes the mapping/landscape aspect. "Atlas of governance actors."
5. **governplay.org** — Leads with the distinctive play/simulation angle that sets this apart.
